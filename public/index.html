DelTxt - Simple & Free Notepad

  • private and free cloud storage.
  • no user tracking, ads, or cookies.
  • data is never shared or sold.
  • old notes are deleted (7 days since last edit).
  • simple interface like Notepad/TextEdit.
  • complete source code: https://github.com/yschermer/deltxt

Why are old notes deleted?
When was the last time you opened your old notes? For most of them, the answer is likely never.
So, declutter your mind and save a tiny bit of the planet by having it automatically deleted.
